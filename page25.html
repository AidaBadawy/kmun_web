Zetech University

Formed around February 15th and approved officially by the KenyaMUN around March, the Zetech KenyaMUN chapter consists of 14 secretariat members and 58 chapter members (currently). 

Zetech University Chapter

The chapter is aimed at impacting students with skills such as communication skills, negotiation skills and public speaking skills especially through internal conferences (within the school) and external conferences (under the KenyaMUN) which will serve as a platform for the youth to air out their opinions on issues that affect the society as well as the whole world especially in the context of globalization.

The Zetech KenyaMUN will practically carryout activities that will act as a simulation of the KenyaMUN under the UN General Assembly as well as the secretariat.

We believe that the future is youth and that through carrying out activities such as communal work, charity organization and tree planting as well as appreciating the important dates of the KenyaMUN calendar we will have achieved this theme.

Being new members in the KenyaMUN fraternity we strongly believe that we will impact many as well as create safe spaces for the upcoming young generation.

Chapter Meetings:
On normal occasions we hold our meetings on Mondays, from 3pm - 5pm which have breaks at every interval. The meetings are held at lecture room 310 or the auditorium sometimes.

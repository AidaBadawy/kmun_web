Moi Annex

One thing defines us at Annex; our resilience and stamina to endure challenges. We are known to be a powerhouse in respect to moot court competitions and debates both nationally and internationally. 

Moi (Annex) Chapter

The Chapter is a student-funded and has over 60 registered members, 33 being active members. Despite having been in existence for over 10 years, the club has had its fair share of challenges including lack of support from the school administration.

The challenges above notwithstanding, the club has been actively participating in the Kenya Model UN conference and other reputable moot court competitions.

The current local secretarial is closely working with the immediate former secretariat and the school administration to improve the impact of the chapter not only to its members but also to the local community.

Our vision going forward is to increase member participation in National Conferences at the same time consolidating our membership base at the chapter level by organizing more debates and conferences. We desire to host one of the Mock Debates for the 20th Session.

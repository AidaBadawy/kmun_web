Our main objective is to nurture individuals into world class diplomats and future leaders. The chapter is located at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, Karen Campus.

JKUAT Karen Chapter

The chapter exposes the members to various issues, both local and global, during our meetings to help find the answer to a very important question which is, what can we as KenyaMUN members do to make a difference?

The chapter has been growing and has a total of fifty two members who actively participated in the events held both in and out of school in the 19th session and look forward to the 20th session. The members of the General Assembly meet every Wednesday from 8:30am to 10:30am, those of the International Court of Justice meet every Tuesday from 3:00pm to 5:pm and those of the International Law Commission meet every Monday from 3:00pm to 5:00pm at the moot court in the school of law. Where they sharpened not only their life skills such as confidence and public speaking among others but also on their knowledge on various issues affecting the society at large.

UoN (Mombasa)

The University of Nairobi (Mombasa Campus) Chapter is a student-run organization emulating the activities of the United Nations inspired by the 17 Sustainable Developments Goals, with our chapter being based in The University of Nairobi, at Uni Towers in Mombasa.

COMMISSIONER'S HANDBOOK:
RULES ON DRAFT ARTICLES

A Draft Article is a Commissioner’s research work on the topic at hand. It is usually marked by the Head Commissioner(s). It can be equated to a Position Paper in the General Assembly.

COMMISSIONER'S HANDBOOK:
RESOLUTION WRITING GUIDELINES

A resolution is the tangible product of a committee's deliberations. It is a committee’s statement on an issue and generally includes suggestions or instructions to member states, UN bodies, and/or other international actors regarding the topic at hand.
